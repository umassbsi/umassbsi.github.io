who we are

let us introduce ourselves

We're a group of undergraduate students that are tired of the way business is done on campus. While we all come from different backgrounds and fields of study, ranging from Computer Science to Biochemistry, we're united in our goal of building a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship here at UMass.

If that sounds good to you, fill out the form above and join us in making this school as great as it can be.

what we do

working with the top hustlers on campus

In short, we're a catalyst for making cool things happen on campus. Our vision is to create a lasting impact on this university by driving students to build and maintain a culture that embraces challenge, change, and creativity. We work directly with the Berthiaume Center for Entrepreneurship to develop the best programs on campus to push innovation.

We assist student-led groups and initiatives in their growth and organization; however, that's only one half of the story. We try to ensure that these groups are set up so that they can thrive well after the original founders have moved on or graduated.
